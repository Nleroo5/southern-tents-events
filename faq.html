Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

General Questions

How far in advance should I book my event rentals?

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for weddings and events during peak season (April-October). Popular dates can book up 6-12 months in advance. However, we often accommodate last-minute requests based on availability. Contact us as soon as you have a date in mind!

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve the entire region and surrounding areas. Delivery fees may apply based on your location. Contact us with your zip code for specific delivery information.

Do you offer delivery, setup, and takedown services?

Yes! Delivery is included with all rentals. Our experienced team delivers all items directly to your event location. Setup fees apply to larger tents to ensure professional installation and proper safety standards. All rental items are picked up after your event is complete.

Can I see the equipment before I book?

Yes! We're happy to show you photos of our inventory and discuss your specific needs. We can share images of our tent styles, table settings, chair options, and more. Contact us to discuss your event requirements.

Tent Rentals

What size tent do I need for my event?

Tent size depends on your guest count and planned activities. As a general guide:

• Cocktail/standing reception: 6-8 sq ft per person
• Seated dinner (no dance floor): 10-12 sq ft per person
• Seated dinner with dance floor: 12-15 sq ft per person
• Add extra space for buffet tables, bar, DJ/band area

Our team will help you determine the perfect size during your consultation.

What if it rains on my event day?

Our tents are designed to protect against rain and provide shade from the sun. All tent tops are made from high-quality, waterproof materials. We can also add clear or solid sidewalls for additional protection from wind and rain. For severe weather, we monitor forecasts and work with you to ensure guest safety.

Do I need a permit for a tent?

Permit requirements vary by location and tent size. In many areas, tents over certain square footage require permits. We're familiar with local regulations and can guide you through the permit process. We recommend checking with your local municipality or HOA early in the planning process.

Can tents be set up on any surface?

Tents can be installed on grass, concrete, asphalt, and other surfaces. Grass is ideal and allows for traditional staking. For hard surfaces like concrete or asphalt, we use weighted systems or water barrels. We'll conduct a site visit to assess your location and determine the best installation method.

Do you offer tent walls or sidewalls?

Yes! All of our tents can be equipped with weatherproof sidewalls that include entrances and windows for an additional fee. Tent walls provide protection from wind, rain, and cooler temperatures while maintaining visibility and airflow. They're perfect for events during unpredictable weather or cooler seasons.

Pricing & Payment

Are there delivery and setup fees?

Delivery fees apply to all orders and are based on your event location. Setup fees are charged for larger tents to ensure safe, professional installation by our experienced crew. Both fees will be clearly itemized in your personalized quote. We never have hidden charges - everything is transparent upfront.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing varies based on the specific items, quantities, event date, and delivery location. We provide detailed, itemized quotes with no hidden fees. Contact us with your event details for a personalized quote. Visit our Services page for current pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), and bank transfers. A deposit is required to secure your reservation, with the balance due before delivery.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! For larger events, we offer flexible payment options. Typically, a 50% deposit secures your date, with the remaining balance due 7-14 days before your event. Contact us to discuss a payment schedule that works for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30+ days before the event receive a full refund minus a small processing fee. Cancellations within 30 days may be subject to fees depending on the timing and items rented. We understand that circumstances change and work with clients when possible. Please refer to your rental agreement for specific terms.

Day-Of & Logistics

When will items be delivered and picked up?

Tent installation typically occurs 1-2 days before your event, depending on size and complexity. Other rental items are usually delivered the day before or morning of your event. Pickup occurs the day after (or Monday for weekend events). We provide specific delivery windows in advance.

Do I need to be present for delivery and pickup?

You or a designated representative should be present for delivery to show our crew the setup location and address any questions. For pickup, if rentals are left in an accessible location, you don't need to be present. We'll confirm logistics before your event.

What if I need to add or change items after booking?

We understand that event details can change! You can modify your order up to 14 days before your event, subject to availability. Contact your event coordinator to make changes. Last-minute additions may be possible depending on inventory.

Are you insured?

Yes, we carry comprehensive general liability insurance. Certificates of insurance can be provided to venues upon request. We also recommend that clients consider event insurance for additional protection.

What happens if rental items are damaged?

Normal wear and tear is expected and included. However, clients are responsible for damage beyond normal use or lost items. We inspect all returns and will notify you of any damage charges. Most issues can be avoided with reasonable care of the equipment.
