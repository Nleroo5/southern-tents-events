Terms & Conditions

Please review our rental policies and guidelines

Delivery & Setup

Our professional team handles all delivery, setup, and takedown to ensure your event runs smoothly.

  • Setup Location: Tents must be set up on grass. We cannot set up on concrete, asphalt, or other hard surfaces.
  • Access Requirements: The setup area must be accessible for our delivery vehicle and crew. Please ensure gates are unlocked and pathways are clear.
  • Underground Utilities: Renters must call 811 to mark all underground utilities (water, gas, electric, sewer lines) before our arrival. We are not responsible for damage to unmarked utilities.
  • Property Condition: We take care to minimize impact on your property, but minor ground impressions from stakes and equipment may occur.
  • Delivery Window: Delivery and setup times will be confirmed prior to your event date. Please allow flexibility for scheduling.
  • Weather Delays: Severe weather may affect delivery schedules. We will communicate promptly if delays occur.

Important Note: Once tents are installed, they must remain in place for the duration of your rental period. Do not attempt to move or dismantle equipment yourself.

Deposits & Cancellations

Deposit Requirements

  • A 50% deposit is required to secure your reservation and rental date.
  • Deposits are non-refundable once your booking is confirmed.
  • Your rental date is not reserved until we receive your signed contract and deposit payment.

Cancellation Policy

  • If you need to cancel your event, your deposit is non-refundable.
  • We understand that circumstances change, but deposits secure our equipment and schedule for your specific date.
  • In certain cases of extreme weather or emergencies, we may work with you to reschedule your event (subject to availability).

Deposit Policy Summary: 50% deposit required | Non-refundable after booking confirmation | Secures your event date and equipment

Payment Terms

To ensure a smooth rental experience, please note the following payment requirements:

  • Initial Deposit: 50% deposit required at booking to secure your reservation.
  • Final Payment: The remaining balance is due 5 days prior to your event date.
  • Payment Methods: We accept cash, checks, Venmo, Zelle, and major credit cards.
  • Late Payment: Equipment will not be delivered if final payment has not been received 5 days before the event.
  • Additional Charges: Any additional items or services requested after booking will be invoiced separately.

Permits & Permissions

It is the renter's responsibility to ensure all necessary permits and permissions are obtained:

  • Property Permission: You must have permission from the property owner to install temporary structures and equipment.
  • HOA/Community Rules: If your event is in a neighborhood with an HOA, verify that tent rentals and events are permitted.
  • Local Permits: Some municipalities require permits for temporary structures. Check with your local government.
  • Venue Requirements: If renting at a public park or venue, obtain all required permits and provide us with any site-specific requirements.
  • Utility Marking: Call 811 at least 48 hours before your event to mark underground utilities (gas, water, electric, sewer).

Renter's Responsibility:

Southern Tents and Events is not responsible for obtaining permits or verifying property permissions. Any fines or issues related to unpermitted installations are the responsibility of the renter.

Responsibility for Damages

Renters are responsible for the care and proper use of all rental equipment during the rental period.

Your Responsibilities

  • Use all equipment safely and for its intended purpose only.
  • Do not move, dismantle, or alter installed tents or structures.
  • Protect rental items from damage, theft, and misuse.
  • Ensure your guests treat rental equipment with care.
  • Report any damage or issues to us immediately.
  • Return all items in the same condition as received (normal wear and tear excepted).

Damage & Replacement Costs

  • Normal wear and tear is expected and acceptable.
  • Renters will be charged for damage beyond normal use.
  • Lost, stolen, or unreturned items will be charged at full replacement cost.
  • Excessive stains, burns, or tears may result in replacement charges.
  • Equipment will be inspected after your event; any damage will be documented and invoiced.

Important: You are financially responsible for any damage, loss, or theft of rental equipment during your rental period. We recommend informing your guests to treat all items with care.

Renter Expectations

To ensure a successful rental experience, we ask that renters adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Site Preparation: Ensure the setup area is clear, accessible, and free of obstacles before our arrival.
  • Underground Utilities: Call 811 to mark utilities at least 48 hours prior to delivery.
  • Access: Gates, driveways, and pathways must be clear for our delivery vehicle and equipment.
  • Tent Safety: Do not attempt to move or dismantle tents. They must remain in place for the entire rental period.
  • Equipment Care: Treat all rental items with care and use them for their intended purpose only.
  • Cleanliness: Remove all food, debris, and trash from rental items before pickup. Excessive cleaning may result in additional fees.
  • Weather Awareness: In case of severe weather (high winds, storms), secure loose items and notify us if any equipment is damaged.
  • Communication: Inform us immediately of any issues, damages, or concerns during your rental period.
  • Pickup Readiness: Ensure all rental items are ready for pickup at the scheduled time and location.
